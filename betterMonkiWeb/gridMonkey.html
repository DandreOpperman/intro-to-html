Orangutan

There are many things that you need to know about the Orangutan, they are fast and cunning and you would be wise not to underestimate them. Although many look cute, this is a facade that masks a primal and ominous power. Orangutans have been known to imitate human speech and bribe zoo visitors with cigarettes, please do not fall for their wicked schemes.

Ventriloquism

For a highly vocal species, golden snub-nosed monkeys have a curious aspect to their communications: they can vocalize without moving their bodies or face, much like a skilled ventriloquist.

Special ability

Dynamic group hunting

During a group hunt, chimpanzee in hunting groups have been known to take on a specific role for the duration of the hunt. "Drivers" serve to keep the prey running in a certain direction and follow them without attempting to make a catch. "Blockers" are stationed at the bottom of the trees and climb up to block prey that takes off in a different direction. "Chasers" move quickly and try to make a catch. Finally, "ambushers" hide and rush out when a monkey nears.
